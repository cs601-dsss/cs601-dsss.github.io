General Information¶

The course will cover the state-of-the-art techniques and principles for designing single node scalable systems software. The course will cover lectures, paper discussion, and a semester-long project focusing on scalability. Note that some of the topics are tentative and we can also cover topics that students want to explore or learn about.

Prerequisite¶
